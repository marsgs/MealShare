Meal Share

Caring for our community one meal at a time

Meal Share was created with the homeless population in mind, mainly their accessibility to food. Many individuals who are homeless struggle to get meals that are healthy or fresh.

Meal Share is here to aid in this problem by receiving fresh food donations from the community, these are foods that would otherwise go to waste. By providing meals to the homeless, at a very small fee, they can worry less about their next meal and more on matters more important.

Want to be a Meal Share recipient?

Go to your local homeless shelter or community center to sign up for the service.
